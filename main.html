About

David Lyman left a rewarding career as a CPA to pursue a passion for fitness. He trained clients at the Island Club and Spa in Honolulu before returning to his Seattle home with his wife. He was recently Fitness Director for LAB5 Fitness, a boutique Pilates studio, and he is now training people just like you, in the convenience of your home, apartment, or business, through Progressive Gym!

When not training clients, he enjoys running, cycling, hiking, dancing, and all things active.
